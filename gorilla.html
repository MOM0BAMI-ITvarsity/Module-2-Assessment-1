GORILLA

Explore the fascinating world of gorillas, the largest living primates, known for their incredible strength, intelligence, and social behavior. Gorillas share about 98% of their DNA with humans, making them our closest relatives in the animal kingdom.

These majestic creatures inhabit the dense forests of central Africa, living in cohesive family groups led by a dominant silverback. Gorillas are herbivores, mainly consuming leaves, fruits, and vegetation.

One of the most remarkable aspects of gorillas is their complex social structure. Family units, known as troops, exhibit strong bonds and communication through vocalizations, gestures, and expressions.
