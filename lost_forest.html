LOST FOREST

Embark on a journey through the mystical Lost Forest. This lush and serene area showcases the beauty of nature and the diversity of flora and fauna found in the heart of the zoo.

Key Features:

  • Walking Trails
  • Botanical Gardens
  • Waterfall Oasis
  • Wildlife Observation Points

Don't miss the daily guided walks led by our expert naturalists, offering insights into the unique plant and animal life thriving in the Lost Forest.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The entrance to the Lost Forest is located near the Butterfly Pavilion. Follow the enchanting trails to immerse yourself in the tranquility of this magical environment.
