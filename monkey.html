MONKEY

Monkeys are a diverse group of primates, known for their agility, intelligence, and playful behavior. There are various species of monkeys found in different parts of the world.

Monkeys are generally social animals, living in groups called troops. They have dexterous hands and feet, allowing them to grasp objects and swing from trees. Many species of monkeys are omnivores, feeding on a variety of fruits, leaves, insects, and small animals.

These intelligent animals use vocalizations, facial expressions, and body language to communicate with each other. Some species of monkeys are known for their impressive acrobatics and problem-solving abilities.
