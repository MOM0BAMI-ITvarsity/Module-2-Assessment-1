MONKEY TRAIL

Experience the thrill of watching monkeys swing through the trees and showcase their playful antics at the Monkey Trail. This immersive habitat provides a glimpse into the social and active lives of our primate residents.

Attractions:

  • Tree-Top Platforms for Viewing
  • Interactive Feeding Sessions
  • Educational Monkey Talks
  • Playful Monkey Enclosures

Join our guided tours to learn more about the different monkey species and their behaviors. Don't forget to bring your camera!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail starts near the Primate Pavilion. Follow the elevated walkway to get close to the action and enjoy a unique perspective of the monkey habitats.
