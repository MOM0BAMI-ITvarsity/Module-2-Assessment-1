PANDA

Pandas are large bears native to China, known for their distinctive black-and-white fur and bamboo-based diet. They are considered a symbol of conservation efforts and are adored worldwide.

Pandas primarily eat bamboo, but they are omnivores and can consume small mammals and birds occasionally. They have a unique thumb-like structure, a modified wrist bone, which helps them grip bamboo while eating.

Pandas are solitary animals and are known for their relaxed and gentle nature. They spend a significant amount of time eating and resting. Despite their large size, they are skilled climbers and swimmers.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

Visit the Panda Pavilion, a tranquil area with lush bamboo where pandas can be seen munching on bamboo shoots and lounging.
