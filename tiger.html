TIGER

Tigers are powerful and majestic big cats, known for their distinctive orange fur with black stripes. They are the largest cat species and are native to various habitats in Asia.

As carnivores, tigers are apex predators and primarily hunt ungulates such as deer and wild boar. They are solitary animals, with males and females coming together only during mating season.

Tigers have powerful limbs and can swim well, enjoying the water. Unfortunately, many tiger species are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
